Explanation of the dataset

Prior to setting up the network of artists it is crucial to understand the data foundation of the network. As already mentioned the data for this study is based on the wikipidedia lists of important artists wihtin each of the five genres. The wikipedia lists can be found here for each genre, Pop, Hiphop, RnB, Rock, Country.

The data has been downloaded by reasing the list of artists for each genre using the wikipedia API.
